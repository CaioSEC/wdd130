Overview

Purpose

The purpose is to serve a broad audience, from those most passionate and dedicated to rafting to fathers and mothers who want to provide a moment of leisure for their family. Therefore, it will be essential to present the company's services in a clear and intuitive way so that even those who are unfamiliar with rafting can feel confident and safe when doing it.

Audience

Who are the target customers? Describe their life (or business) situation (age, financial situation, interests, and so on)

Adults interested in water rafting, whether experienced or not

What do they want?

Entertainment, adventure, leisure and creating good memories.

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?

Detailed information about the services the company offers through photos, details of requirements and precautions, as well as other essential stuff.
